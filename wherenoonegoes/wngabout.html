Where no one goes is an ongoing comicproject, created by Akiro Tea.

It started out as a shortstory about this couple who's just met and in order to get to know each other, the girl invites the boy to take a step inside her head to get to know her through her memories.

Vol. 2 tells the story of one of those memories, that we only get a short glimpse of in Vol. 1, in more detail.

Vol. 3 is currently in the making and will be available around January 2019.

There are 4 Volumes planned in total.

Where no one goes is a selfpublished and independent comic project. It's only financially supported through comicbook sales and patreon. If you enjoy the comic, please consider to support! You can find all the ways to contribute on the SUPPORT page. Thank you!
