If you‘re a comic writer and think my art

would suit your writing, you can hire me as a comic artist

You can e-mail me your order at akirotea@protonmail.com Before you do so, please read the Terms & Conditions. They contain all the information about my working process and how to order.

To give you an accurate price estimate I‘ll need to know the following:

1. What do you want? > Just lineart or colour? What style do you have in mind? Do you have examples? > Will you need a cover? > What is the setting and genre? > How many characters are involved? Are there animals? A script would be ideal.

2. Specifications. Examples: > How many pages do you need? > What are the page dimensions? (A4, A5, …, do you have a template?)

3. What is it for? Examples: > Is it for print or web? Children or adults? For education or entertainment?

4. What is your Deadline.

I‘ll get back to you with a price estimate as soon as possible.
