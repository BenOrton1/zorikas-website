My name is Zorika Gaeta.

I was born and raised in southern Germany near the rhine river where after graduating from middle school, I got a degree in graphic-design. Later I moved to Berlin to study at the the Film University Babelsberg Konrad Wolf. I graduated in 2018 with a BFA in 2D Animation

At present I live in Leeds, England, creating independent comics and working as a freelance artist for comic, illustration and 2D animation.

I enjoy walking, laughing and making friends with dogs.
